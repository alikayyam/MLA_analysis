\section{Per-Layer Metric Tables (Experiment~1)}
\label{app:per-layer-tables}

Tables~\ref{tab:v2lite-layers}--\ref{tab:v3-layers} report the per-layer
Gram distance $D_\text{Gram}$ and mean principal angle $\bar\theta$ (in
degrees) for all three models.
$D_\text{Gram}$ is defined as
$\|\hat{G}_K - \hat{G}_V\|_F$ where
$\hat{G}_X = \widetilde{W}_X\widetilde{W}_X^\top /
              \|\widetilde{W}_X\|_F^2$
and $\widetilde{W}_X = W_c W_X^\uparrow$.
$\bar\theta$ is the mean of the $r$ canonical principal angles between
$\operatorname{Col}(\widetilde{W}_K)$ and
$\operatorname{Col}(\widetilde{W}_V)$, computed via thin-QR followed by
SVD of the cross-Gram.
DeepSeek-V3 weights were converted from FP8 to \texttt{bfloat16} before
metric computation.
